Stardust Blitz

•3D Art
•UI Animation

Stardust Blitz is a side-scrolling shooting game with a 3D background and 2D foreground, where players fly through space and cosmic bases to fight waves of enemies.

Team Size: 6
Engine: Unreal Engine 5

I was responsible for creating the 3D background models for Stardust Blitz. My goal was to capture a retro pixel-art style using 3D, and I developed a complete workflow for producing pixel-like textures. I also helped establish a consistent pipeline that allowed the 3D modeling team to collaborate more efficiently.

The Ones Left Behind

•3D Art
•Unreal Blueprint

The Ones Left Behind is a narrative-driven game about the aftermath of a young girl’s suicide, focusing on how her friends and family respond to her absence. Players explore various scenes, interact with objects, and uncover the story through dialogue and environmental storytelling.

Team Size: 6
Engine: Unreal Engine 5

I was responsible for the first-person character animations and some of the character models in the game. I also implemented interactive object highlights based on the player’s line of sight in Unreal Engine 5, and blueprint for scene transitions.





The Traffic Wizard

•2D Art

The Traffic Wizard is a week-long production Demo where players use magic to direct traffic at a busy intersection.

Team Size: 13
Engine: Godot/Unity
